# Phase 0 — Validation Plan

**Duration:** 2–4 weeks  
**Status:** In progress  
**Goal:** Confirm demand, pricing, and MVP scope before Phase 1 build

## Objectives

1. Validate that solo psychologists feel the pain points described in [Problem Statement](../product/problem-statement.md)
2. Confirm willingness to pay RD$700–1,200/month for MVP features
3. Prioritize features via workflow interviews (not assumptions)
4. Secure pilot commitments for Phase 1 launch

## Interview Targets

| Segment | Target | Priority | Purpose |
|---------|--------|----------|---------|
| Independent psychologists | 20 | Primary | Validate MVP workflows and pricing |
| Therapy center admins | 5 | Signal only | Confirm Phase 3 demand; do not scope MVP |

## Recruitment

- Personal network and referrals in Santo Domingo
- Psychology professional associations and university alumni groups
- LinkedIn / Instagram outreach to private practice psychologists
- Offer: early access + discounted pilot pricing in exchange for feedback

## Interview Structure

Use [Interview Guide](interview-guide.md) — 30–45 minutes per session:

1. Warm-up and practice context (5 min)
2. Current workflow walkthrough (15 min) — map to our three workflows
3. Pain ranking exercise (5 min)
4. Concept reaction — show MVP scope summary (10 min)
5. Pricing sensitivity (5 min)
6. Pilot interest and close (5 min)

## Deliverables

- [ ] 20 psychologist interview notes
- [ ] 5 center admin interview notes
- [ ] Updated [Hypothesis Log](hypothesis-log.md) with evidence
- [ ] Confirmed MVP scope (any cuts or additions)
- [ ] Pricing model validated
- [ ] 3 pilot customer commitments (name + contact)

## Success Criteria (Go to Phase 1)

| Criterion | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Psychologists expressing interest | ≥ 10 |
| Pilot commitments | ≥ 3 |
| Pricing validated | Majority accept RD$700–1,200/month range |
| Top pain confirmed | Scheduling, reminders, or payments ranked #1 by majority |
| WhatsApp channel confirmed | Reminders via WhatsApp preferred over email/SMS |

## Go/No-Go Decision

After 20 interviews (or 4 weeks, whichever comes first), founders review hypothesis log and decide:

- **Go:** Proceed to Phase 1 MVP build with confirmed scope
- **Pivot:** Adjust persona, pricing, or feature set and run 10 more interviews
- **Stop:** Insufficient demand or willingness to pay

## Timeline

| Week | Activities |
|------|------------|
| 1 | Recruit participants; conduct first 5–8 interviews |
| 2 | Conduct 8–10 more interviews; begin hypothesis log updates |
| 3 | Complete remaining interviews; 5 center admin calls |
| 4 | Synthesize findings; go/no-go decision; update docs |

# Vision

## Mission

Help psychologists spend less time managing their practice and more time helping patients.

## What NexusOne Is

NexusOne is a SaaS platform for psychologists and therapy centers in the Dominican Republic, starting in Santo Domingo. It combines practice management (patients, scheduling, notes, payments, reminders) with a long-term path toward patient acquisition through a therapist marketplace.

## Near-Term (Phase 1 MVP)

Become the default practice management tool for **independent psychologists** in Santo Domingo who currently juggle WhatsApp, Google Calendar, Excel, and paper notes.

## Mid-Term (Phases 2–3)

- Recover lost revenue from no-shows via Smart Waitlist
- Support therapy centers with multi-therapist and room management
- Provide actionable reporting (revenue, attendance, no-shows)

## Long-Term (Phases 4–7)

| Phase | Capability |
|-------|------------|
| Marketplace | Public therapist profiles, discovery, commission-based bookings |
| Teletherapy | Video sessions, secure chat, file sharing |
| AI Layer | Session summaries, practice insights, scheduling optimization |
| LATAM Expansion | Colombia, Mexico, Chile — localization, multi-currency, compliance |

## End State

The operating system for mental health professionals in Latin America — where therapists run their practice, acquire patients, and deliver care (in-person and online) from one platform.

## Strategic Sequence

```
Practice Management SaaS
        ↓
Therapy Centers
        ↓
Online Booking
        ↓
Therapist Marketplace
        ↓
AI-Powered Mental Health Platform
```

# Personas

## Primary: Dr. Ana — Independent Psychologist

**Role:** Solo private practice psychologist  
**Location:** Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic  
**Patients:** 20–100 active  
**Tech comfort:** Uses smartphone daily; relies on WhatsApp for patient communication

### Profile

Ana sees 4–6 patients per day, mostly recurring weekly sessions. She charges per session (RD$1,500–3,000) and tracks payments informally. She has no assistant.

### Goals

- Spend less time on scheduling and admin
- Never lose track of who paid and who didn't
- Have session notes organized and searchable
- Reduce no-shows without manual reminder work

### Frustrations

- Patients cancel via WhatsApp with little notice; slot goes empty
- Payment tracking in Excel is always out of date
- Can't find notes from sessions 3 months ago quickly
- Google Calendar doesn't connect to patient records

### How NexusOne Helps (MVP)

- Unified calendar linked to patient profiles
- Session notes attached to appointments
- Payment status per session with monthly revenue view
- Automatic WhatsApp reminders (24h and 2h before)

### Validation Focus

Interview Ana-types to confirm: Which pain is worst? Would she pay RD$700–1,200/month? Does she already have an informal waitlist?

## Secondary (Future): María — Therapy Center Administrator

**Role:** Administrative manager at a therapy center  
**Location:** Santo Domingo  
**Center size:** 5–15 therapists, 3–6 shared rooms  
**Status:** Documented for Phase 3 — not in MVP or Phase 0 interview priority

### Profile

María schedules therapists into rooms, handles front-desk intake, and reports monthly occupancy to the center owner. She does not write clinical notes.

### Goals

- See all therapists' schedules in one view
- Assign rooms without double-booking
- Generate center-level revenue and attendance reports
- Give therapists access without exposing other therapists' notes

### Why Future-Only

MVP targets the solo psychologist workflow. Center workflows introduce multi-tenancy, role-based access, and room entities that add significant scope. We interview 5 center admins in Phase 0 for **signal only** — to confirm demand without building for them yet.

# NexusOne

Mental Health Practice Management Platform for Psychologists and Therapy Centers.

## Mission

Help psychologists spend less time managing their practice and more time helping patients.

---

# Features

## Patients

- Patient profiles
- Session history
- Secure records
- Document management

## Scheduling

- Calendar management
- Recurring sessions
- Appointment reminders
- Smart waitlists

## Financial

- Payment tracking
- Revenue dashboards
- Outstanding balances

## Communication

- WhatsApp reminders
- Cancellation workflows
- Waitlist notifications

---

# Long-Term Vision

NexusOne will evolve into a complete mental health ecosystem including:

- Practice management
- Patient acquisition
- Teletherapy
- AI assistance
- Marketplace
